Platters Meats and Sides

At Fiesta Empire, we take pride in offering authentic Mexican cuisine made with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.

Choose from our pre-designed platters or create your own taco/nacho bar with your option of two meats, two sides, and any toppings!

Plattered Entrees

  • Plato de Quesadillas – Authentic Mexican quesadillas made with melted Oaxaca cheese and your choice of chicken, beef, or both, elegantly prepared for sharing.
  • Plato de Enchiladas – Rolled tortillas filled with seasoned chicken or beef and topped with rich red enchilada sauce and our house white sauce.
  • Plato de Tamales – A selection of authentic tamales (chicken, pork, or cheese with poblano peppers), served with salsa.

Taco and Nacho Bar Options

Meats

  1. Ground Beef –A slow-simmered blend of ground beef, onions, and tomatoes, infused with earthy cumin, smoky paprika, and a touch of heat.
  2. Shredded Chicken Tinga – Tender shredded chicken simmered in a smoky chipotle-tomato sauce. A crowd favorite with a hint of heat and bold flavor.
  3. Carne Asada – Marinated flank steak grilled to perfection, sliced thin for easy serving. Rich, savory, and packed with authentic Mexican flavor.

Sides

  • Mexican Rice – Fluffy rice cooked with tomato, garlic, and spices.
  • Refried Beans – Creamy pinto beans mashed and seasoned with garlic and spices.
  • Elote (Mexican Street Corn) – Grilled corn off the cob mixed with mayo, cotija cheese, lime, and chili powder. Sweet, smoky, and tangy.

Toppings & Add-ons

  • Fresh Pico de Gallo – A zesty mix of diced tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and lime juice.
  • Guacamole – Creamy avocado blended with lime, cilantro, and a touch of jalapeño.
  • Sour Cream – Cool and tangy, perfect for balancing spicy flavors.
  • Black Beans – Whole black beans simmered with onions and spices.
  • Shredded Cheese – A blend of cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses, great for melting over nachos or tacos.
  • Lettuce & Cabbage – Crisp shredded lettuce or cabbage for a refreshing crunch.
  • Jalapeños – Sliced fresh or pickled jalapeños for those who like it hot.
  • Hot Sauces – A variety of salsas and sauces ranging from mild to fiery–ask us about our house-made options!

Note: All ingredients are customizable to meet dietary preferences.
